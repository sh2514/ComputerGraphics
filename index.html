Final Project (in progress)
  • Platform game with multiple levels.
  • WAD or arrow keys to move. A and D to move left or right. W to jump.
  • Hold A or D to run and accelerate to jump higher and further.
  • Press E to laugh.
  • Left click and hold to tilt level.
  • If you experience lag, close some browser windows or applications running.

Note: Level backgrounds and soundtracks are from Softnyx's Gunbound and Hal's Kirby. Character model based on Teemo from Riot's League of Legends. First level design, background, and soundtrack are based on or are from Nintendo's Mario games.
